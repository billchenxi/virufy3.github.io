Decoding hidden patterns in COVID-19 coughs with AI

Virufy is a volunteer-run organization uniting the world to build a global artificial intelligence (AI) database of crowdsourced cough sounds to identify patterns that signify respiratory diseases, such as COVID-19.

Our supporters

How Virufy works

Record your cough and contribute to the global AI database.

Virufy is giving everyone across the world a chance to be part of the solution by sharing their unique coughs, which will be used to train the machine learning models. Donate your cough now!

AI can recognize respiratory disease signatures in cough sounds

COVID-19 damages the throat and lungs, causing microvariations in pitch, tone, and other audio signals, which machine learning can detect. Learn more in our research page.

A consortium of AI researchers will build the solutions.

Virufy is bringing together researchers from across the globe to expedite the solution by pooling together resources and knowledge. Please see our open data page
